# Glyiv

Glyiv is a **carbon-intelligence platform** that helps people understand the climate impact of daily spending and turn that insight into real action.

## What Glyiv Does

- Converts shopping receipts into estimated carbon impact using AI OCR and classification.
- Gives users a personal carbon tracking experience with a clear, visual dashboard.
- Builds toward an ecosystem where carbon tracking, compensation, and green payment rails work together.

## Product Vision

### Phase 1: Personal Carbon Assistant
- Scan receipts or input purchases manually.
- Calculate estimated emissions per transaction.
- Build healthy low-emission habits through progress and gamification.

### Phase 2: Compensation Marketplace
- Connect land owners and offset participants.
- Introduce tokenized carbon-compensation participation.
- Expand incentives for consistent low-impact behavior.

### Phase 3: Green Payment Infrastructure
- Integrate carbon-aware payment and green receipts.
- Support broader merchant and institutional adoption.
- Move toward a scalable digital public green-finance layer.
